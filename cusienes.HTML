Chicken Biriyani

Chicken Biriyani is a famous dish in india

Chicken biryani is a flavorful rice dish popular in South Asia, known for its spices, chicken with masala , and layered cooking method.

The Chicken Biriyani Was Brought to India by Mughal Emperors, it is also believed that a trader brought the dish concept from Afghanistan.

The Ingredients needed to make chicken Biriyani

rice, chicken (thighs or breasts), yogurt, ginger,garlic paste, onions, tomatoes, garam masala, mint, coriander and Chicken biriyani masala

chicken biryani is always nice with some raita on the Side!
